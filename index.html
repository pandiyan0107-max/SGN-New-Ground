Why It Matters

More Than Just A Ground

🧒

Youth Development

A dedicated space gives young people a safe place to play, build discipline, and develop sporting talent right in their own neighbourhood.

🏃

Health & Fitness

Regular physical activity reduces stress, builds stamina, and promotes long-term health for children and adults alike.

🤝

Community Bonding

Shared spaces create shared memories. A local ground becomes a hub where families and neighbours come together.

🌙

Night Play Option

With optional floodlights, working adults and students can enjoy sport in the evenings after their day responsibilities.

🏆

Talent & Tournaments

A proper ground enables local tournaments, inter-colony matches, and opportunities to spot and nurture local talent.

🔒

Safe Environment

A fenced, dedicated space keeps children away from traffic and provides parents peace of mind during playtime.

Project Scope

What Will Be Built

Every rupee contributed goes towards a clearly defined scope of construction. Here's exactly what we're building:

🔗

GI Chain Link Fencing

8 gauge, 2-inch mesh · 12 ft height chain link + 3 ft safety net on top

🏗️

MS Poles & Foundation

Mild steel poles with concrete foundation for structural stability

⚽

Goal Post Installation

Standard 5-a-side goal posts, properly anchored and positioned

📐

Ground Leveling & Marking

Surface preparation and pitch line marking for a playable surface

💡

Lighting (Optional)

Floodlights for evening and night play — if funding permits

📏

Ground Dimensions

75 ft × 75 ft · 5-a-side / 6-a-side format · Full perimeter fencing
